About Apollo :
Apollo Hospitals is a leading healthcare provider in India, with over 10,000 beds across 73 hospitals. The Apollo Hospitals Group was founded in 1983 by Dr. Prathap C Reddy, and is known for pioneering the private healthcare revolution in India. Apollo Hospitals is the first corporate hospital in India, and was the first to invest in international quality accreditation. The group has also developed centers of excellence in cardiac sciences, orthopedics, neurosciences, emergency care, cancer, and organ transplantation. Apollo Hospitals has five core values: empathy, integrity, responsibility, unity, and pioneering.
